## Description — English

Ever discovered your car insurance expired last week? Or that your vehicle inspection was due last month?

DocDue helps you track all documents with deadlines — insurance, personal IDs, bills, contracts — everything in one simple, elegant app.

KEY FEATURES:

• Category organization — Vehicles, Home, Personal, Financial — with predefined subtypes for common documents (insurance, inspections, utilities, passports, contracts and many more)

• Health Score — A visual 0-100 score showing instantly if you have expired or soon-to-expire documents

• Smart alerts — Contextual notifications before expiration, with relevant tips for each document type

• Morning digest — A daily notification with your score and documents that need attention

• Color coding — Red (expired), orange (due soon), green (all good) — see status at a glance

• OCR scanning — Take a photo of a document and auto-fill title, amount and date

• Calendar sync — Add deadlines directly to your phone calendar

• Biometric lock — Face ID / Touch ID to protect your data

• Excel export — Export and import documents in Excel format (PRO)

• Backup — Create and restore data backups (PRO)

100% PRIVATE
Data stays on your phone. No servers, no tracking, no ads. DocDue works completely offline.

Available in Romanian and English.
