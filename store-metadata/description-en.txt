APP NAME: DocDue - Document Tracker

SUBTITLE (30 chars max):
Track Deadlines. Stay Ahead.

SHORT DESCRIPTION (Google Play, 80 chars):
Track document expiration dates. Never miss a deadline for insurance, IDs, bills.

LONG DESCRIPTION:

Never miss a deadline again. DocDue keeps track of all your important document expiration dates in one place — insurance policies, IDs, driver's licenses, utility bills, contracts, and more.

KEY FEATURES

- Smart Reminders: Get notified days before any document expires. Morning digest and weekly summaries keep you informed.
- Four Categories: Organize documents by Vehicle, Home, Personal, and Financial — each with dozens of preset subtypes.
- Quick Add with OCR: Snap a photo and DocDue auto-fills the date, amount, and document type using on-device text recognition.
- Payment Tracking: Mark documents as paid, track payment history, and see monthly spending analytics.
- Recurring Documents: Set up annual, monthly, or weekly recurrence. When you pay, the due date automatically advances.
- Health Score: See your overall document health at a glance — know instantly if anything needs attention.
- Calendar Integration: Add due dates directly to your device calendar with one tap.
- Bilingual: Full Romanian and English support. Switch anytime in settings.
- Biometric Lock: Protect your documents with Face ID, Touch ID, or fingerprint.
- Backup & Restore: Export your data as a backup file. Import it on any device.
- Home Screen Widget: See upcoming deadlines without opening the app.
- Dark Design: Beautiful dark interface inspired by modern fintech apps.

PRIVACY FIRST

DocDue stores everything on your device. No accounts, no cloud, no tracking, no ads. Your data never leaves your phone.

Free with up to 5 documents. Upgrade to PRO for unlimited documents, analytics, backup/restore, and Excel export.
