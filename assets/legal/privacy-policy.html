Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

DocDue ("we", "our", or "the app") is a document and expiration date tracking application. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data Collection

DocDue stores all your data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers. All documents, settings, and preferences remain exclusively on your phone.

2. What Data is Stored

The app stores the following data locally on your device: document titles, due dates, amounts, category assignments, recurrence settings, notes, and your app preferences (theme, language, currency, warning threshold). None of this data leaves your device.

3. No Account Required

DocDue does not require you to create an account, provide an email address, or sign in. The app is fully functional without any personal information.

4. No Third-Party Services

The current version of DocDue does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. No data is shared with third parties.

5. Notifications

DocDue may send local notifications to remind you about upcoming document expirations. These notifications are generated entirely on your device and do not involve any external servers.

6. Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time from the Settings screen within the app. Uninstalling the app will also permanently delete all stored data.

7. Children's Privacy

DocDue is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at: docdueapp@gmail.com
